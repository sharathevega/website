Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 7 days before the event for a full refund. After that, 50% refund applies.

Is there a dress code?

Business casual attire is recommended. Comfortable shoes are suggested as the venue is spacious.

Will meals be provided?

Yes, lunch and refreshments are included for all attendees. Special dietary requirements can be accommodated - please mention in registration.

Can I attend just one day?

Yes, single-day passes are available for $199. Select your preferred day during registration.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes, all keynote sessions will be recorded and available to registered attendees for 30 days after the conference.

Is there parking available?

Yes, the convention center has on-site parking. Early arrival is recommended as spaces fill up during the day.
